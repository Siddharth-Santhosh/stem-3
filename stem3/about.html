About STEM.3

STEM.3 is a digital textbook project created to help KS3 students (ages 11–14) see how physics and engineering ideas show up in everyday life. Instead of dry notes, each section uses stories, diagrams, and challenges to make concepts stick.

The project is inspired by platforms like open courseware and modern learning tools, but intentionally stays lightweight: no heavy frameworks, just clean HTML, CSS, and a small amount of JavaScript for progressive enhancement.

Design & Learning Approach

  • Professional tone — clear, exam‑aligned explanations without talking down to learners.
  • Visual thinking — diagrams, infographics, and simple animations to support the text.
  • Accessibility first — high contrast, keyboard‑friendly navigation, and readable typography.

Future Features

The roadmap for STEM.3 includes:

  • An AI learning assistant that can answer questions, suggest revision tasks, and track progress.
  • Interactive labs and simulations for topics like forces, circuits, and energy transfer.
  • A quiz engine with class leaderboards and, where possible, integrations with tools like Google Classroom or Microsoft Teams.

Authorship & Collaboration

Content is written to match KS3 level while still stretching curious learners. If you are a teacher, student, or partner interested in contributing or piloting STEM.3 in your school, please use the contact page to get in touch.
