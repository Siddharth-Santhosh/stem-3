What is STEM.3?

STEM.3 combines clear explanations, story‑driven examples, and future interactive tools (AI tutor, labs, and quizzes) to help students truly understand core physics and engineering ideas.

Project Goals

  • Professional, educational design with strong readability.
  • Simple navigation and fast loading on any device.
  • Accessible to WCAG 2.1 AA standards.
